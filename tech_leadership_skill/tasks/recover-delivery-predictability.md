# Recover Delivery Predictability

Use this when roadmap is slipping, teams are blocked, incidents disrupt delivery, agile is ritualized, or executives distrust commitments.

1. Establish current state.
   - Verify: commitments, actual delivery, blockers, incidents, dependencies, scope changes, and capacity are visible.
2. Identify failure mode.
   - Verify: decision latency, dependency drag, capacity overload, unclear ownership, quality debt, security gate, or vendor blocker is named.
3. Stabilize WIP.
   - Verify: active work is reduced or prioritized by business risk and critical path.
4. Reset commitments.
   - Verify: new commitments include confidence, assumptions, trade-offs, and escalation path.
5. Fix operating cadence.
   - Verify: blockers, dependencies, risk, and decisions have weekly review and owners.
6. Include reliability and cyber work.
   - Verify: incident load, on-call, security remediation, and technical debt are not hidden outside capacity.
7. Communicate recovery.
   - Verify: executives, peers, and teams understand what changed and how progress will be measured.

Output: recovery plan, dependency map, commitment reset, risk register, and dashboard.
