# Task: Select Governance, Compliance, Or Delivery Framework

## Goal
Decide whether to use ITSM/ITIL, COBIT, TOGAF, ISO/IEC/IEEE, PCI DSS, PMBOK, CMMI, SOC 2, CNCF, Gartner Magic Quadrant, HIPAA, GDPR, PDP, SAFe, Lean Six Sigma, DORA/MTTR, another framework, or no framework.

## Required Inputs
- Business objective and decision needed.
- Sector, geography, customers, contracts, and audit context.
- Data classes and critical business processes.
- Delivery model, team count, service ownership, and operating maturity.
- Current pain: outage, audit gap, vendor uncertainty, project drift, process variation, governance weakness, or platform complexity.

## Steps
1. State the strongest reason not to add a framework.
2. Classify the problem driver: governance, architecture, service management, compliance, project control, process improvement, cloud-native platform, vendor market scan, privacy, or delivery performance.
3. Map candidate frameworks using `references/framework-selection-and-critical-prompts.md`.
4. Identify verification items for laws, standard editions, certification scope, vendor claims, and jurisdiction.
5. Define minimum viable implementation, decision rights, evidence, metrics, operating cadence, adoption cost, and misuse risk.
6. Recommend one path: adopt, adapt, defer, combine lightly, or reject.

## Verification
- Framework has a named owner, budget, evidence path, and decision use.
- Scope and non-scope are explicit.
- Legal/regulatory applicability is not guessed.
- Ceremony and documentation are proportional to risk.
- Vendor or market research is not treated as architecture proof.

## Output
Use `templates/framework-selection-decision-record.md` and optionally `templates/mermaid-framework-selection.mmd`.
