# Evaluate AI, Data, R&D, Or Innovation Initiative

Use this for AI initiatives, data products, R&D proposals, technology research, prototypes, or innovation portfolio review.

1. Define problem and hypothesis.
   - Verify: business problem, user, artifact, and expected improvement are explicit.
2. Check readiness.
   - Verify: data, infrastructure, security, privacy, governance, talent, and operating ownership are assessed.
3. Compare alternatives.
   - Verify: non-AI or lower-complexity options are considered.
4. Define experiment.
   - Verify: method, sample, success threshold, risk boundary, duration, and stop criteria are written.
5. Evaluate risk.
   - Verify: cyber, privacy, model safety, reliability, cost, adoption, and regulatory risk are included.
6. Plan scale path.
   - Verify: production ownership, monitoring, support, cost, and governance are addressed.
7. Report learning.
   - Verify: decision is adopt, iterate, pause, or stop with evidence.

Output: experiment plan, evaluation matrix, risk review, and investment recommendation.
