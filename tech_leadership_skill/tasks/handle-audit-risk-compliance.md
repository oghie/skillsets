# Handle Audit, Risk, And Compliance

Use this for audit preparation, risk review, compliance remediation, control design, policy, or management response.

1. Identify obligations.
   - Verify: framework/regulation/customer contract/internal policy is named; current applicability requires live verification.
2. Map controls to owners.
   - Verify: each control has owner, evidence, frequency, system, and exception process.
3. Assess evidence readiness.
   - Verify: evidence is available, repeatable, and not manually invented after the fact.
4. Build risk register.
   - Verify: likelihood, impact, control status, owner, due date, and risk acceptance path are included.
5. Prioritize remediation.
   - Verify: materiality, audit due date, cyber risk, customer impact, and operational cost are considered.
6. Prepare management response.
   - Verify: response is factual, owned, dated, and measurable.
7. Establish ongoing governance.
   - Verify: cadence, dashboard, exception review, and executive reporting are defined.

Output: audit readiness pack, risk register, policy/control map, remediation plan, and management response.
