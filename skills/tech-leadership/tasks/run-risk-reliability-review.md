# Run Risk And Reliability Review

Use this for high-risk systems, recurring incidents, hidden operational risk, burnout risk, control weakness, or leadership reviews where risk cannot be reduced to a compliance register.

1. Define the risk surface.
   - Verify: business process, critical system, people group, customer impact, and risk appetite are explicit.
2. Separate visible event from hidden contributors.
   - Verify: weak signals, near misses, workaround patterns, lucky successes, and unreported concerns are listed.
3. Map system controls.
   - Verify: barriers, redundancies, and recoveries are named and tested where possible.
4. Assess human reliability.
   - Verify: behavior is classified as error, at-risk choice, reckless choice, or intentional harm; outcome bias is checked.
5. Assess organizational reliability.
   - Verify: incentives, culture, staffing, vendor dependence, external pressure, and leadership messages are examined.
6. Design reporting and learning loop.
   - Verify: trusted reporting, triage, acknowledgement, analysis owner, and corrective action path exist.
7. Decide treatment.
   - Verify: action, owner, due date, residual risk, evidence, and executive acceptance path are written.

Output: reliability review, risk-control map, behavior/context assessment, and corrective action plan.
