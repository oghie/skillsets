# Build Performance Management System

Use this for KPI design, executive dashboard, balanced scorecard, performance reporting, operating reviews, or when metrics exist but decisions do not improve.

1. Anchor to strategy.
   - Verify: objectives, KRAs, critical success factors, and accountable executive are written.
2. Define metric system.
   - Verify: each KPI has formula, owner, source, cadence, threshold, and decision trigger.
3. Build scorecard cascade.
   - Verify: executive decision metrics, managerial diagnostic metrics, team metrics, and individual-accountability boundaries are separated.
4. Establish review rhythm.
   - Verify: team, department, executive, and board cadences have clear outputs.
5. Define RCA and improvement path.
   - Verify: metric misses route to 5 Whys, fishbone, FMEA, current reality tree, DMAIC-style review, or qualitative inquiry as appropriate.
6. Control incentive distortion.
   - Verify: Goodhart risk, gaming risk, false precision, individual productivity proxies, and punitive use of diagnostic metrics are addressed.
7. Ship dashboard and reporting template.
   - Verify: dashboard shows trend, variance, narrative, decision needed, owner, action, and due date.

Output: performance management map, KPI dictionary, dashboard, review cadence, and improvement backlog.
