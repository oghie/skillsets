# Quantify Technology Leadership System

Use this when a CTO, VP Engineering, Head of Engineering, or tech leader needs executive metrics, managerial metrics, board scorecards, operating dashboards, or clearer quantification of technology value, risk, capacity, and management health.

1. Split metric altitude.
   - Verify: executive decision metrics and managerial diagnostic metrics are separated.
2. Map decisions.
   - Verify: each metric has a decision owner, decision trigger, and action path.
3. Quantify business and portfolio.
   - Verify: value, run/grow/transform/protect allocation, roadmap confidence, and realized ROI are represented.
4. Quantify risk and resilience.
   - Verify: reliability, cyber/IAM, audit/compliance, architecture debt, vendor concentration, and data/AI risks are included where material.
5. Quantify people and manager system.
   - Verify: manager span, skip-level signal, attrition, engagement, succession, onboarding, feedback, delegation, and burnout risk are visible.
6. Control metric harm.
   - Verify: individual productivity proxies, vanity metrics, false precision, and incentive distortion are identified.
7. Build dashboard.
   - Verify: trend, target, confidence, interpretation, decision ask, owner, and next review are included.

Output: executive-managerial quantification map, KPI dictionary, metric risk review, and dashboard skeleton.
