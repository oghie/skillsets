# Build Technology Roadmap

Use this for short-term, mid-term, long-term, platform, modernization, technical debt, resilience, scalability, or transformation roadmaps.

1. Define business outcome.
   - Verify: objective is measurable and tied to customer, revenue, cost, risk, compliance, or mission.
2. Inventory demand and constraints.
   - Verify: product, operations, security, compliance, technical debt, reliability, and infrastructure needs are visible.
3. Build portfolio allocation.
   - Verify: run/grow/transform/protect capacity is explicit.
4. Sequence by dependency and risk.
   - Verify: critical path, integration points, data/security controls, and operational readiness are included.
5. Add decision gates.
   - Verify: each major initiative has entry criteria, exit criteria, owner, funding, and stop/pivot condition.
6. Define metrics.
   - Verify: roadmap success includes business, delivery, reliability, cybersecurity, finance, and people indicators where relevant.
7. Prepare communication.
   - Verify: executive summary, team narrative, and stakeholder-specific trade-offs are ready.

Output: roadmap, investment view, risk register, and communication plan.
