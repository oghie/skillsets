# CTO 30/60/90-Day Diagnostic

Use this for a new CTO, acting CTO, tech leader entering a new mandate, or advisor diagnosing an organization.

1. Establish context.
   - Verify: sector, geography, business model, regulation, budget authority, reporting line, team size, technology estate, and current commitments are known or listed as questions.
2. Map stakeholders and decision rights.
   - Verify: CEO/board/executive peers/product/security/risk/audit/customer/vendor/team expectations are separated.
3. Identify material risks.
   - Verify: delivery, cybersecurity, reliability, people, financial, compliance, vendor, and architecture risks have owners or gaps.
4. Review operating evidence.
   - Verify: roadmap, incidents, metrics, budget, hiring, attrition, audit findings, security posture, cloud/vendor spend, and technical debt are inspected.
5. Build 30-day stabilization plan.
   - Verify: critical blockers, incidents, cyber gaps, decision bottlenecks, and communication gaps have immediate owners.
6. Build 60-day operating model improvements.
   - Verify: cadence, dashboard, governance, portfolio allocation, and manager expectations are defined.
7. Build 90-day strategy and investment view.
   - Verify: roadmap, risk appetite, budget asks, org changes, and board/executive narrative are ready.

Output: diagnostic summary, risk register, roadmap, stakeholder map, operating cadence, and next decisions.
