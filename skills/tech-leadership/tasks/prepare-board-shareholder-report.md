# Prepare Board Or Shareholder Report

Use this for board packs, shareholder updates, executive committee, risk committee, or investor-facing technology reports.

1. Determine audience and decision need.
   - Verify: the report has a clear ask, decision, or oversight purpose.
2. Summarize current state.
   - Verify: business outcome, roadmap progress, material changes, and major risks are clear.
3. Include technology portfolio.
   - Verify: run/grow/transform/protect work and funding implications are visible.
4. Include cybersecurity and resilience.
   - Verify: residual cyber risk, incident readiness, audit findings, control maturity, and major exceptions are summarized if material.
5. Include people and organization.
   - Verify: leadership capacity, hiring, retention, succession, and critical capability gaps are stated.
6. Include financials.
   - Verify: budget variance, forecast, major vendor/cloud spend, and investment asks are explicit.
7. Define next decisions.
   - Verify: decision owner, deadline, options, trade-offs, and recommended path are stated.

Output: board report with executive summary, scorecard, risks, decisions, and appendix candidates.
