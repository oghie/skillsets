# Conduct Performance And 360 Calibration

Use this for appraisal, promotion, performance improvement, succession, compensation calibration, or 360 review.

1. Define role expectations.
   - Verify: level, scope, outcomes, behavior, and leadership expectations are explicit.
2. Gather evidence.
   - Verify: outcomes, collaboration, technical judgment, reliability/security ownership, mentoring, stakeholder feedback, and continuous feedback notes are included.
3. Separate performance from potential.
   - Verify: current delivery and future capability are evaluated separately.
4. Check bias and visibility.
   - Verify: recency, proximity, politics, heroics, invisible work, cultural communication style, remote visibility, and manager preference are challenged.
5. Calibrate across peers.
   - Verify: similar scope and impact receive consistent ratings.
6. Define development or corrective plan.
   - Verify: next expectations, support, timeline, and review criteria are written.
7. Prepare conversation.
   - Verify: feedback is direct, evidence-based, behavior-specific, fair, and not a surprise.

Output: appraisal summary, 360 matrix, calibration notes, continuous feedback summary, development plan, and risk watchlist.
