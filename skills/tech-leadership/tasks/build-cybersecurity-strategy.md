# Build Cybersecurity Strategy

Use this for cybersecurity strategy, cyber roadmap, CISO/CTO alignment, board cyber reporting, risk appetite, security operating model, or audit-driven remediation.

1. Define business and threat context.
   - Verify: sector, crown jewels, critical processes, data sensitivity, geography, regulatory exposure, and likely threat profile are captured.
2. Clarify accountability.
   - Verify: CTO, CISO, CIO, legal, risk, audit, product, platform, and business owner responsibilities are explicit.
3. Establish risk appetite.
   - Verify: unacceptable risks, risk acceptance authority, critical asset baseline, and escalation thresholds are defined.
4. Assess capability domains.
   - Verify: IAM/PAM, GRC, SOC/detection, incident response, AppSec, cloud/infrastructure, data security, vendor risk, backups/recovery, and awareness are covered.
5. Prioritize roadmap.
   - Verify: stabilization, baseline, integration, optimization, and strategic trust work are sequenced by asset criticality and business impact.
6. Define metrics and board view.
   - Verify: KPIs/KRIs show residual risk, control coverage, remediation age, incident readiness, audit findings, exceptions, and funding gaps.
7. Build audit and incident readiness.
   - Verify: control evidence, tabletop plan, crisis communication, recovery evidence, and exception process exist.

Output: cyber strategy brief, cyber risk register, roadmap, dashboard, accountability map, and verification list.
