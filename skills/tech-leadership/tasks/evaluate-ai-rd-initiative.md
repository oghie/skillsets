# Evaluate AI, Data, R&D, Or Innovation Initiative

Use this for AI initiatives, data products, R&D proposals, technology research, prototypes, or innovation portfolio review.

1. Define problem and hypothesis.
   - Verify: business problem, user/customer, artifact need, hypothesis type, prediction, and expected improvement are explicit.
2. Check readiness.
   - Verify: data, infrastructure, security, privacy, governance, talent, and operating ownership are assessed.
3. Compare alternatives.
   - Verify: non-AI or lower-complexity options are considered.
4. Define AI/data operating model.
   - Verify: data product/customer value, agent autonomy boundary, model/tool permissions, human-in-the-loop, and escalation are clear.
5. Define experiment.
   - Verify: method, sample, success threshold, risk boundary, duration, validity threats, and stop criteria are written.
6. Evaluate risk.
   - Verify: cyber, prompt injection, data leakage, model poisoning, privacy, model safety, reliability, cost, adoption, responsible AI, and regulatory risk are included.
7. Plan scale path.
   - Verify: production ownership, observability, fallback, release strategy, support, cost, and governance are addressed.
8. Report learning.
   - Verify: decision is adopt, iterate, pause, or stop with evidence.

Output: experiment plan, AI/data/R&D evaluation matrix, risk review, ROI model, and investment recommendation.
